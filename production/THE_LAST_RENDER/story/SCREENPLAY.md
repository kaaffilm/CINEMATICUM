# THE LAST RENDER — Screenplay

## EXT. INDUSTRIAL SERVICE ROAD — NIGHT

Rain crosses sodium light. A lone technician approaches a concrete facility at the edge of the service road.

## EXT. FACILITY ENTRANCE — NIGHT

The security light flickers above a sealed metal door. The technician opens the access panel. The door releases only halfway.

## INT. CORRIDOR — NIGHT

Dead monitors repeat the same unfinished render frame. Water drips through ceiling panels. The technician moves deeper.

## INT. STAIRWELL — NIGHT

Red emergency light. Concrete. Breath. Descent.

## INT. MACHINE ROOM — NIGHT

A central render machine glows like a cold furnace. The technician stands small before it.

## CLOSE ON SWITCH

The hand hesitates.

The switch falls.

## INT. MACHINE ROOM — CONTINUOUS

The room tears into broken projection. Smoke. Red-white rupture. Then blackout.

## INT. MACHINE ROOM — LATER

Fans spin down. A dead screen holds only reflection.

## EXT. FACILITY EXIT — DAWN

The technician exits into pale rain.

The facility remains.
