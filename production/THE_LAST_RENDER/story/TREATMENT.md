# THE LAST RENDER

A technician enters an abandoned render facility after the final automated render jams in an infinite loop.

The film is not about an interface, not about a proof object, and not about procedural abstraction. It is a grounded physical short: rain, concrete, human scale, machine presence, silence, and dawn release.

The camera must always prove material reality: wet ground, imperfect light, moving body, lens compression, dust, metal, cable, glass, breath, and hesitation.
