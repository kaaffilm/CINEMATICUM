# Forbidden Image Doctrine — THE LAST RENDER

The following images are inadmissible even if technically impressive:

- generic neon science fiction
- AI woman walking through a city
- one-prompt trailer montage
- beautiful shot with no dramatic function
- unmotivated camera drift
- fake terminal screens used as decoration
- dialogue scene carried by exposition
- image that explains the proof chain instead of turning it into story

A lawful image must change evidence, pressure, knowledge, fear, sacrifice, or recognition.
